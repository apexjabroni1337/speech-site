SPEECH

Privacy Policy

Effective June 2026 · applies to the SPEECH browser extension and website

SPEECH is an open comment layer for the web. This is what we store and why.

Account data. Your email address and a hashed password, used only for signing in. Your username, display name and bio are public.

Content. Comments, votes, friend connections, group memberships, interest tags, and reports you submit are stored in our database. Comments, votes, profiles and groups are public — that is the point of the product.

Page addresses. When someone comments on a page, we store that page's address and title so others can find the thread. To show comment counts, the extension checks the current page's address against our database; addresses are only stored permanently once a page has been commented on. The extension never reads or collects the content of pages you visit.

What we don't do. We do not sell or share personal data, run ads, or use third-party trackers or analytics. Data is hosted by Supabase.

Deletion. You can delete your own comments in the extension. For full account deletion, contact us via the email on our store listing and we will remove your account and personal data.

Changes to this policy will be posted on this page.
